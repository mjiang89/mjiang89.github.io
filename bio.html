Bio

I am a postdoctoral researcher of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Jiawei Han. I joined the Data Mining Research Group in August 2015.

I completed my Ph.D. in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, Beijing under the supervision of Professor Shiqiang Yang in 2015. I did my bachelor degree at the same department in 2010. I was born in Lianyungang (a city by the Sea), Jiangsu Province, China.

I visited the Database Group in School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Christos Faloutsos from August 2012 to May 2013. We have collaborated on 7 papers including one as ACM SIGKDD 2014 Best Paper Finalist.

My research focuses on mining and modeling user behaviors in social media. Problems I investigate vary from prediction & recommendation to suspicious behavior detection.
