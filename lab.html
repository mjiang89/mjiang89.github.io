DM2 Lab: Data Mining towards Decision Making

"We develop data-driven computational methods and intelligent systems to facilitate human decision making." -- dm_square

Data-driven Behavior Analytics: Model behavior intention, social and spatio-temporal context, and unstructured text content, and consolidate into real use such as recommender systems, anti-fraud systems, and mission planning systems.
  • [Content] Information extraction and natural language generation: Given text content (news, tweets, articles), can we discover factual information, construct knowledge graphs, and generate natural language text?
  • [Context] Representation learning: Given heterogeneous behavior data structures, can we fuse the context and content information for prediction and recommendation? Can we model the high-dimensional evolutionary patterns?
  • [Intention] Suspicious behavior detection: Can we develop actionable solutions to preventing individuals from misinformation and misbehavior, such as spam, fraud, bots, fake news, influence campaigns, coordinated attacks, and cyberbullying?
