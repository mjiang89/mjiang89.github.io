DM2 Lab: Data Mining towards Decision Making

"Build trustworthy intelligent systems to support decision-making process with rich knowledge in massive behavior data." -- dm_square

Data-driven User Behavior Modeling: Understand behavior intention, model social and spatio-temporal context, represent unstructured behavior content, and consolidate into real use such as recommender systems, anti-fraud systems, and event monitoring systems.
  • [Content] Text mining: Given text content (news, tweets, papers), can we discover factual knowledge and generate information-rich structures in an unsupervised manner?
  • [Context] Representation learning: Given heterogeneous behavior data structures, can we learn representations of the context and content information for prediction and recommendation?
  • [Intention] Suspicious behavior detection: Can we use data science to find effective actionable solutions to preventing individuals from misbehavior and threat, such as spam, fraud, bots, coordinated attacks, cyberbullying, and others?
