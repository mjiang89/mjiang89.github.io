Meng Jiang 蒋 朦

I am a postdoctoral researcher of Computer Science in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Jiawei Han. I completed my Ph.D. in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, Beijing with Dissertation Award in 2015. I visited Professor Christos Faloutsos at Carnegie Mellon University in 2013 and visited Professor V.S. Subrahmanian at University of Maryland Institute for Advanced Computer Studies, College Park in 2016. [C.V.]

My research focuses on developing data-driven approaches of online behavior analysis for applications such as future behavior prediction, social recommendation, fraud detection, and event summarization, called Data-Driven Behavioral Analytics. It is at the intersection of social media analysis, spatiotemporal data mining, text mining, information extraction, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
