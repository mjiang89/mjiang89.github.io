Meng Jiang

I am a postdoctoral researcher of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Jiawei Han. My research focuses on modeling user behaviors and mining social media. Problems I investigate vary from prediction & recommendation to suspicious behavior detection.

What's new

  • Coming in November: Tutorial on Behavior Modeling in Social Networks: From Micro to Macro with Dr. Peng Cui at ICDM 2015.
  • November: A paper about cross-platform behavior modeling was accepted by AAAI 2016. We proposed Semi-Supervised Transfer Learning to address the problem. See you at Phoenix, Arizona, in February 2016!
  • August, 2015: I am excited to join the Data Mining Research Group, Data and Information Systems (DAIS) Research Laboratory, Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign!
