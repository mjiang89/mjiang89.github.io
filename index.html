Meng Jiang

I am a postdoctoral researcher of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Jiawei Han. My research focuses on modeling user behaviors and mining social media. Problems I investigate vary from prediction and recommendation to suspicious behavior detection.
