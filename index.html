Meng Jiang

I am a postdoctoral researcher of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Jiawei Han. My research focuses on Data-Driven Behavioral Analytics with Networks. Problems I investigate vary from prediction and recommendation to suspicious behavior detection.

Given massive behavior data (user-item interaction, e.g., purchasing, tweeting, paper-publishing), I study

  • Mining user-item behavior network of social, spatiotemporal contexts and suspicious intentions;
  • Structuring rich, heterogeneous information network from unstructured items' content;
  • Integrating behavior network and information network for intelligent and trustworthy systems.
