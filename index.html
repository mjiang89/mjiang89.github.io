Hi. I'm Meng Jiang (蒋朦).

I am now a Postdoctoral Research Associate of the Data Mining Research Group in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (advised by Professor Jiawei Han). I graduated with a PhD from Tsinghua University (advised by Professor Shiqiang Yang and Peng Cui. My research interests are behavior modeling and social network analysis. My CV and resume are here.
