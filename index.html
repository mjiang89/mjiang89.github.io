Meng Jiang

I am a postdoctoral researcher of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Jiawei Han. My research focuses on mining and modeling user behaviors in social media. Problems I investigate vary from prediction, recommendation to fraud detection.
